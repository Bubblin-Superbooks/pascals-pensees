_Contradictions._—The sceptre till the Messiah—without king or prince.

The eternal law—changed.

The eternal covenant—a new covenant.

Good laws—bad precepts. Ezekiel.

Types._—When the word of God, which is really true, is false literally, it is true spiritually. _Sede a dextris meis: this is false literally, therefore it is true spiritually.
