But to show you that this leads you there, it is this which will lessen the passions, which are your stumbling-blocks.

_The end of this discourse._—Now, what harm will befall you in taking this side? You will be faithful, honest, humble, grateful, generous, a sincere friend, truthful. Certainly you will not have those poisonous pleasures, glory and luxury; but will you not have others?
