Principles of Rabbinism: two Messiahs.

Will it be said that, as men have declared that righteousness has departed the earth, they therefore knew of original sin?—_Nemo ante obitum beatus est_—that is to say, they knew death to be the beginning of eternal and essential happiness?

[_Miton_] sees well that nature is corrupt, and that men are averse to virtue; but he does not know why they cannot fly higher.
